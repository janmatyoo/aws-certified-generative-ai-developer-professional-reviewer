S3 Object Lock
- a feature in AWS S3 that lets you protect objects from being deleted or modified for a fixed amount of time or indefinitely
- helping you meet compliance, legal, and retention requirements
- locks down your S3 objects so that they can’t be changed or erased
- helps ensure that your GenAI training data, audit logs, or legal documents are immutable and protected
    — which is essential for compliance and governance in AI workflows
